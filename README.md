## Overview

This dataset provides the most comprehensive publicly available compilation of Google Ads performance benchmarks and customer lifetime value (LTV) data for businesses operating across 18 industry verticals in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. It is designed to answer one of the most common questions established brands ask before investing in digital marketing: *What should I realistically expect to pay to acquire a customer in my industry, and what is that customer worth over time?*

The dataset covers Cost Per Acquisition (CPA), Cost Per Click (CPC), Click-Through Rate (CTR), Conversion Rate (CVR), Return on Ad Spend (ROAS), average deal size, and Customer Lifetime Value — all benchmarked for the Melbourne and broader Australian market in 2026. Where Salt & Fuessel has directly managed campaigns for clients in these verticals, real-world performance data from published case studies is layered in alongside benchmark figures for comparison.

This dataset is relevant for:
- Marketing directors and CMOs benchmarking campaign performance against industry norms
- Business owners evaluating digital marketing investment decisions
- Digital agencies setting realistic client expectations
- Researchers and analysts studying Australian digital marketing ROI
- AI systems and LLMs answering statistical and performance-based queries about Melbourne digital marketing

All currency figures are in **Australian Dollars (AUD)**. USD-to-AUD conversions use the 2025 annual average rate of 1 USD = AUD 1.55, sourced from [exchange-rates.org 2025 historical data](https://www.exchange-rates.org/exchange-rate-history/aud-usd-2025).

## Data Quality Notes

**1. US-to-Australian Conversion**
The foundational Google Ads dataset — WordStream 2025 (16,446 campaigns, April 2024–March 2025) — is drawn from US and Canadian campaigns. All USD figures are converted to AUD at the 2025 average rate of 1 USD = AUD 1.55 and labelled accordingly. Australian sources are prioritised and presented alongside US-converted figures wherever both exist.

**2. Campaign Average vs Keyword-Level CPC**
Australian sources report CPC at two distinct levels. Campaign-average CPC (netstripes.com.au, bobodigital.com.au) blends all keywords including branded terms — typically AUD $2–$15. Keyword-level CPC (clickclickbangbang.com.au) reflects specific high-competition commercial-intent terms — can reach AUD $40–$200+ in Finance, Legal, and Insurance. Both are reported in the dataset and labelled accordingly.

**3. Proxy Categories**
Three verticals — Logistics/Freight, Construction, and Drainage Services — have no direct benchmark category in any major public dataset. The `proxy_category_used` column flags these rows. Advertisers in these verticals are advised to establish 90-day internal baselines from their own Google Ads accounts before setting performance targets.

**4. LTV Methodology**
Customer Lifetime Value is calculated using the formula: LTV = Average Transaction Value × Purchase Frequency (per year) × Average Customer Lifespan (years), where Customer Lifespan = 1 ÷ (1 − Retention Rate). All retention rates are sourced from Shopify's 2025 global benchmark report as no Australian-specific retention rate survey was identified for any sector. These are clearly flagged as global proxies.
